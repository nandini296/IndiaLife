Cultural event on the occation of Gudi Padwa was held in the city

Location, April 9, 2023

'Chaitrapalvi' a dance event was held on the occasion of Gudi Padwa on behalf of Kalavardhini Sanstha and Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR). Dance Guru Pt Manisha Sathe (Kathaka), and Manjiri Alegaonkar (Classical Singing) participated in this program. The event was organised under the cultural outreach program of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan and Infosys Foundation. This cultural program was planned to mark the arrival of Chaitra month.
